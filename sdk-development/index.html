Your SDK Development Partner

When it comes to building SDK's across iOS and Android our  experience is vast. We can help you formulate the right SDK development strategy and ensure that it's easy for developers to integrate your technology.

We have built SDKs for the following companies:

  • SiteSpect
    Mobile A/B and multivariate testing product that lets publishers create and deploy A/B tests by editing their own apps live, on-device.
  • Localytics
    App marketing and analytics platform that allows developers to collect tens of billions of analytics data points per month, helping them build better products.
  • UserTesting
    Provides actionable usability feedback, including high-fidelity video recordings of users' first impressions and experiences with native apps.
  • AppBlade
    Offers automatic crash reporting and user-initiated bug reports with screenshots so developers can build robust apps. It also supports runtime permissions checks to protect confidential data.
  • Rich Relevance
    Offers related product information for retailers
  • Kwicr
    Gives developers better network performance with media

  • Platform consistent with Swift, ObjectiveC and Android best practices
  • Well tested and fault tolerant
