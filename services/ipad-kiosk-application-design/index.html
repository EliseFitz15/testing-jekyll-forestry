Kiosk Application Development

What started out as a mobile computing platform has found its way into physical environments. Our team has built a number of kiosk iPad applications for venues, entryways, customer service scenarios and self-help situations.

Our design and engineering team has partnered with physical enclosure and hardware companies to design and develop streamlined solutions.

[content_right]Our team has experience building kiosk applications for a variety of situations and scenarios.

  • Integration with hardware printers.
  • Integration with displays via AirPlay.
  • Wall-mounted kiosks.
  • Pedestal kiosks.
  • Integration with retail systems and bar code scanners.
