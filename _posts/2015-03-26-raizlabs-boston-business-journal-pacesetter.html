Incredible Growth

This month, Raizlabs was given the honor of being named a Boston Business Journal Pacesetter. The Boston Business Journal's Pacesetters identifies the area's fastest growing companies.
[/table_cell]
[/table_display]
This year's list includes 70 privately held businesses that recorded the state's fastest revenue growth from 2011 through 2014. To be considered, companies needed a baseline of $1 million in revenue for 2014.

We're excited to be recognized for our tremendous growth recently, and we're even more excited for what the future holds for us.

We have even more plans for massive growth, including a hiring spree and continuing to work with even more awesome clients. Part of our future growth also includes the enormous new office space our company just moved in to, as well as the startup incubator that we're running this summer.

The 2015 Boston Business Journal Pacesetters will be celebrated during an April 16 breakfast event at the Westin Copley Place in Boston that will feature a countdown to the No. 1 fastest-growing company on this year's list.
