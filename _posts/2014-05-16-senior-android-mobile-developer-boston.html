Do more than code. Raizlabs is seeking an experienced senior Android mobile developer to engineer beautiful apps and influence product direction for startups and big brands. We've built winning Android apps for B&H Photo, Bloomingdale’s, Benjamin Moore, RunKeeper, Rue La La, Virgin, Costco and many more.

You’ll be working with enthusiastic and supportive peers in a trust-based work environment.

Join us and contribute to the "next big thing" for years to come.

Skills & Requirements:

We’re looking for seasoned Android mobile developers who love shipping mobile apps. You should understand Android inside and out and take pride in your craft.  Our engineers have built award winning Android products and we hold each other to the highest standards.

Other Perks:

- Full coverage of health and dental insurance premiums for full time employees.

- 401(k) retirement plan with company match.

- Trust-based vacation policy (take what you need).

- Continuing education assistance.

- Frequent tech talks, team lunches, hack days for open exploration, weekly coffee and beer tastings, and our monthly mobile meet-up, "Drinks on Tap."

About Raizlabs:

Raizlabs is a tightly-knit team of mobile experts dedicated to building great software. Companies request our expertise and talent to conceive, design, and engineer their mobile strategies. We’ve worked in a diverse array of fields ranging from education to medical wearables and are always playing with novel technologies.

Our user experience designers and engineers often riff on new ideas and challenge each other, whether to experiment with new interactions or optimize an animation to run "like butter." We work hard to be a collaborative and community-minded workplace.

We don't accept just any project— we respond to every lead and screen them down to the most exciting and challenging. We often pitch projects to each other to ensure there’s a fit. As a result, we deliver high quality apps that we would enjoy to use, ourselves.

Curious about some of our code? Check out some of our work on GitHub and let us know what you think: raizlabs.github.io

Location: Boston, MA (no offsite work or contractors). Principals only. Recruiters, please don’t bother contacting us and do not re-post. Qualified applicants should submit a resume and cover letter.
