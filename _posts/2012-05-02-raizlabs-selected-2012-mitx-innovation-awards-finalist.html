Raizlab’s AppBlade SELECTED AS A 2012 MITX INNOVATION AWARDS FINALIST

AppBlade Recognized for Innovation

Brookline, MA (April 30, 2012) – Raizlabs today announced its AppBlade product has been selected as a finalist in the Best Bootstrapped Start-Up category for the MITX Innovation Awards. Held annually by the Massachusetts Innovation & Technology Exchange, the MITX Innovation Awards celebrate the innovations powering the future of marketing and revolutionizing the way we work and play.

"The 2012 finalists represent some of the best innovators in the region, these forward-thinking companies are developing unique, new solutions to problems that are game changing," said MITX President Debi Kleiman. "We were so impressed by this group during a very competitive year, it was amazing to see the range and depth of new things being created here, and we're very proud to honor their great achievements."

AppBlade was developed by a team of designers and developers at Raizlabs that were frustrated with the lack of great tools to manage and distribute mobile applications. Raizlabs built AppBlade because we needed a set of tools to manage, deploy and create better mobile application.  We want to create amazing tools to keep the phone personal while giving companies the tools they need to secure and manage their workforce.

“We’re excited to have Raizlabs nominated for Best Bootstrapped Start-Up at the MITX Innovation Awards,” said CEO and Founder Greg Raiz. “Our company has been focused on helping others navigate the mobile space. We’re excited about expanding our impact and providing companies with products like AppBlade in addition to design and development services.”  VP of Product Management Jason Petralia added, “AppBlade has been in use for over a year, and our biggest critics have been those using the tool internally in our offices. We’ve been able to create a better product in a way that would have been impossible at any company other than Raizlabs.”

Raizlabs will be recognized with the other finalists in the category of Best Bootstrapped Start-Up at the ceremony attended by the region’s top interactive and technology professionals. Tickets and info regarding the event can be found at www.mitxawards.org/innovation.

 

About Raizlabs

Founded in 2003 by Gregory Raiz, Raizlabs has a focus on building world-class mobile software. We partner with companies, agencies and startups that believe mobile is bigger than just building an app.

Our mission is to help companies navigate the mobile space through strategy, user interface design and development services. Our collaborative process results in easy-to-use applications that deliver superior results. Raizlabs focuses on creating top-selling, award-winning products. http://www.raizlabs.com

About AppBlade

Launched in 2011 AppBlade  is a platform  for enterprise mobile application distribution.  It provides tools for application developers in enterprises to manage and distribute applications. AppBlade is a technology leader in mobile app management.  http://www.appblade.com

About MITX

Established in 1996, MITX -- the Massachusetts Innovation & Technology Exchange -- is the leading industry organization focused on the web and mobile, bringing together the digital marketing, media and technology community to engage in what’s next and how it will impact the marketing and business.
