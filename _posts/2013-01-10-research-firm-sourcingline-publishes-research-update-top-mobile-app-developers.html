Raizlabs has been selected as one of 15 top firms that can be trusted to help clients develop and execute market leading mobile strategies by leading research firm SourcingLine. 

SourcingLine published a revised edition of their Mobile Application Development LeadersMatrix. The Leaders Matrix analyzes each firm’s focus in mobile development against their ability to deliver great applications. This publication adds depth to the SourcingLine’s existing mobile development research, which has been called most comprehensive available online.

Out of the 200 companies that applied to be included in the Mobile Application Developers Leaders Matrix, only 15 firms were selected, including Raizlabs.
