In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about Apple's iPhone 5 and iPod update announcement. We discuss the new features for the iPhone 5, the merits of getting a new iPod Nano or iPod touch and new EarPod headphones. Of course, we also recommend our picks (or deletions) for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Ben Johnson (@scoober), Dave Counts (@davecounts) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Math Puppy - From toddlers to grade school, for children of all ages – Math Puppy is the perfect way to build up your math skills. Your child will be able to enjoy a constructive, supportive, interactive fun filled environment while mastering the arts of basic math. Math Puppy is designed with 3 difficulty levels and 5 game types that provide complete coverage of basic math skills for preschool, elementary school, grade school and more.
  • Tumblr - Follow the world's creators. Post anything: Share photos, video, quotes, chats, links, and text. Multiple blogs: Seamlessly manage all of your blogs. Advanced controls: Save drafts, queue posts, and more! Messages: View and reply to messages. Easy camera access: Swipe the compose button up! Offline support: Post, like, reply, and reblog even when you’re not connected
  • Lift - Lift helps you overcome complacency by enabling you to declare your habit goals and see when you're actually making progress on them. And since Lift is social, you'll get to enjoy the support of your peers and friends, many of whom are trying to form the same sorts of habits!
  • Desk Bell - Can't find anyone to help you? Ring the desk bell! Desk bell is designed to solve all of your customer support problems. Whenever you need someone to be at your beck and call, simply ring the desk bell and someone will be sure to respond! If you really need assistance, try shaking the iPhone to let them know that you mean business.
