A new application has been released that allows users to record audio messages and leave voice mail on Facebook walls. The application was developed by Raizlabs for our client People Operating Technology. The application allows users to easily connect with Facebook, post audio messages to friends and contacts on Facebook. These messages can then be played right within the Facebook website.

Additional information can be found on the People Operating Technology website:
http://audiopeople.peopleoperatingtechnology.com/
