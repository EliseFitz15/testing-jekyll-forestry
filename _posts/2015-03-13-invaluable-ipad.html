a new way to bid live

Invaluable, the world’s largest online auction marketplace of fine arts and collectibles, approached Raizlabs to design and build a top-notch iPad app that would cater to a new wave of online auction goers.

Raizlabs set out to create something unique and bring prestigious live auctions to anyone on mobile.

milliseconds matter

How do you pull off a live auction in a low-network world, where every millisecond matters?

With a team of senior mobile engineers, Raizlabs was able to overcome difficult engineering challenges. By leveraging Invaluable's live-streaming API and maintaining a persistent socket connection during live auctions, Raizlabs was able to simulate a near-instant auction experience. This unique problem solving ability and engineering skillset allowed Raizlabs to deliver a great live auction experience on mobile, where connections aren't always lightning fast.
