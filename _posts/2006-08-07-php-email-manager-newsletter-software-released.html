PHP Email Manager is a web tool that makes it easy for websites to maintain newsletters and subscription lists directly from their site. The new software is compatible with PHP and MySQL and allows users to create and maintain large mailing lists of subscribers.

The software allows users to create multiple mailing lists and send those lists from any computer. The mailing lists can maintain thousands of subscriptions and are currently being used by schools, businesses and large companies to maintain subscriber information.

The new software is priced at $89.99 per server with developer licenses also available. An online demo of the product and additional information is available at:
