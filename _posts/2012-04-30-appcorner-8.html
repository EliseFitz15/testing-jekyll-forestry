In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk about the kickstarted funded project Pebble (an e-paper watch for iPhone and Android), crowdfunding legislation, the launch of Google Drive, AT&T's new iPhone unlock policy, and our app recommendations of the week.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Craig Spitzkoff (@oofoot), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

AppCorner: Episode #8

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • 645 Pro - a camera app for iPhone.
  • Google Drive for Android - Google's new storage service.
  • Leafsnap - an app developed by Columbia University that uses visual recognition software to help identify tree species from photographs of their leaves.
  • CardMunch- an iPhone app that converts business cards into contacts.
  • FIFA '12 - a soccer game for iPhone and iPad.
