In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk about how the Facebook IPO has performed over the first week of trading, Google's acquisition of Motorola's mobile division, and as usual, our app recommendations of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jason Petralia (@jasonpetralia), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • Ticket to Ride Pocket - a digital adaptation of the best-selling Ticket to Ride board game for iPhone.
  • iTranslate Voice - speak into your phone and iTranslate Voice immediately speaks back to you in one of 31 languages available in the app.
  • Let's Sing - a music game that combines karaoke with Name That Tune by having users whistle or hum songs that friends must guess.
  • Swordigo - a side-scrolling game for iPhone that lets you run, jump and fight your way through a vast world to defeat the spreading corruption.
  • Angry Birds Space - the popular series launches the latest iteration of it's series set in space with 70 levels and brand new gameplay features.
