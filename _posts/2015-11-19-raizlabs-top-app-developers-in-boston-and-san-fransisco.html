We're excited to announce that independent research firm Clutch.co has ranked Raizlabs as one of the top app developers in San Fransisco and also one of the top app developers in Boston. Clutch has independently verified and reviewed hundreds of firms. Their methodology is based on references, reviews and client experience. This includes quality of deliverables, cost, reputation, and ability to meet deadlines.

Raizlabs has been building apps from the first day of the App Store and is excited to be included and also leading the category.

The company was founded in 2003 with a focus on software development. Raizlabs has focused on app development with a specialty in iOS, Android and related mobile products. Raizlabs is unique in it's ability to combine top designers, developers and product leaders to build best-in-class products.

Raizlabs has been recognized both locally in Boston by the Boston Business journal for growth and service in IT. Nationally Raizlabs has been also recognized by the Inc 5000 as being one of americas fastest growing companies for two years in a row.
