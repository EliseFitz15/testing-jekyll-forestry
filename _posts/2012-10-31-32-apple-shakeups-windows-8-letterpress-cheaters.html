In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about Apple's firing of Scott Fornstall, the new Windows 8, and we give away the secret to beating your opponent when playing the Letterpress app. Of course, we also recommend our picks for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's regular podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Ben Johnson (@scoober), Craig Spitzkoff (@@oofoot) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Letterpress - Find words, steal tiles, color the board! Letterpress is a fresh new word game for iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch. Play against your friends using Game Center! The perfect blend of fun and strategy.
  • I Cheat at Letterpress - Enter all the words on your Letterpress board and this will return words that can be played. Created by our developer Craig.
  • Languages - Lightning-fast translation that works without an internet connection. Most translation apps require an internet connection. Languages stores all words on the phone itself so you always have translation when you need it, whether you are traveling internationally or simply don't have a connection. 12 complete language dictionaries included.
  • Squarespace Note - Squarespace Note helps anyone record their ideas on the fly. Writers, bloggers, and others can use the app to record inspiration and ideas as they happen; notes can be sent via e-mail, or synced with a range of popular services including Squarespace, Evernote, Dropbox, and more.
  • Google Search for iOS - Search the web faster and easier with the latest Search app from Google. Get new features available only in this app. Now, with streaming voice search.
