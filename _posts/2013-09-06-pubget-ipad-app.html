MAKING A VAST RESEARCH LIBRARY AVAILABLE ANYWHERE.

ALL IN THE NAME OF SCIENCE

Pubget makes scientific research easier by simplifying the process of finding, managing and analyzing scientific papers. The company wanted to develop a native iPad app in an effort to further support the research community.

Getting Results

By expanding their online presence to iPad, Pubget was able to get its content in more places, in front of more users.

How it Happened

Raizlabs took a good look at Pubget.com, the company's primary search tool for content discovery. We wanted to be sure that the iPad app we designed and developed would meet the expectations of Pubget's audience of researchers.

This Library's Always Open

The app includes an easy-to-use navigation screen where users can easily navigate, browse, save and retrieve articles through Pubget’s services. The PDF reader allows users to search, bookmark text and even take notes.

Technology and Service

While building Pubget, Raizlabs provided:

  • Ideation and mobile strategy.
  • iPad development and design.
  • Client / server and API integration.
