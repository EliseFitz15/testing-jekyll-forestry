In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk with two of our developers for an all E3 edition of AppCorner. E3, the Electronic Entertainment Expo, serves as the annual trade fair for the computer and video games industry. Companies such as Microsoft, Nintendo and Sony present their newest hardware and games to consumers and media. This year Microsoft debuted SmartGlass, Nintendo showcased the Wii U and Sony showed off the Move controller and Vita. Additionally, we have our recommendations for apps of the week as usual.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog), Alex Rouse, Nick Donaldson and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and tech discussed in this episode include:

  • Air Mail - take to the skies as a fearless letter-carrying, disaster-averting pilot in this flying game for iPhone and iPad.
  • UEFA Euro 2012 - keeps you close to all the action during the 14th UEFA European Football Championship (from 8 June to 1 July 2012 in Poland and Ukraine). Wherever you are, stay tuned to the tournament, follow your favourite team and share the excitement with your friends on Facebook and Twitter .
  • Facebook Camera - you can share photos on Facebook faster than ever, and see what friends are up to in a feed of nothing but their photos.
  • Hypnotoad - the famous Hypnotoad from the TV show Futurama is now on your Android phone.
