Various elements of the WWDC12 keynote discussed include:

  • 2012 Apple Design Awards - Each year, Apple awards the best and most innovative Macintosh and iOS software and hardware produced by independent developers, as well as the best and most creative uses of Apple's products. Recipients this year included apps previously discussed on the podcast such as Paper and National Parks by National Geographic.
  • Macbook updates - Refreshed Macbook Air, Macbook Pro and the introduction of the next-gen Macbook Pro with retina display. The retina display offers 220 ppi with a 2880 x 1800 resolution.
  • OS X Mountain Lion - The new operating system incorporates Notes, Reminders, Game Center to the Mac, Notification Center, Share Sheets, Twitter integration, AirPlay Mirroring and Dictation in addition to iMessage.
  • iOS 6 - The latest version of iOS includes over 200 features such as Siri including the ability to launch apps, more knowledge of sports, restaurants and movie times, Yelp and Open Table integration, better Facebook integration, a "Do Not Disturb" mode and an updated Maps.
