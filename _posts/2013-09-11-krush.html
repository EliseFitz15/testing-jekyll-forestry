ON-TREND MEANS ON-THE-GO

Krush is a network where users share images of lifestyle-driven products, people and brands. The company sought out Raizlabs to bring their online experience into the hands of its users, allowing for on-the-go discovery of the latest trends.

Getting Results

Raizlabs built a high-quality app that was hot right out of the gate, getting featured as a New and Noteworthy app in the App Store's Lifestyle category and endorsed by pro skateboarder Rob Dyrdek on his own website and Twitter. Krush saw a nice bump in engagement in the process.

How it Happened

We worked to create a visually stunning app that matches the feel of a site dedicated to the curation of product images. We focused on features that mattered most to continuing the social experience of Krush.com into the mobile space.

SNEAKERS TO SURFBOARDS

Krush for iPhone allows users to configure views by category (like skateboarding, tech, style etc.) and favorite or save items to their own personal profiles. Users' activity on the app and website serve to influence what's trending on the Krush network, and their personal profiles allow them to share favorites with friends.

TECHNOLOGY AND SERVICE

In creating the iPhone app for Krush, Raizlabs provided:

  • Ideation and mobile strategy.
  • Native iPhone development and design.
  • Social media integration.
  • Push notifications.
