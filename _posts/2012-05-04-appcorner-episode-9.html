In this week's episode of AppCorner we show off the awesome flash app Incredibox, discuss RIM's unveiling of the Blackberry 10, chat about Microsoft's $300 million investment in the Nook, the importance of enterprise devices and our app recommendations (and fails) of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jon Green (@gildedsplinter), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • Incredibox - a flash music app.
  • Quasar - a windows manager for iOS apps on iPad.
  • HBO Go - HBO's on demand service.
  • Balloonimals - an app developed by Columbia University that uses visual recognition software to help identify tree species from photographs of their leaves.
  • Tweetbot- a Twitter client app for iPhone, iPod and iPad.
  • National Parks by National Geographic - an app showcasing the National Parks by National Geographic.
  • Netflix - the streaming video service's app for iPhone.
  • Pinterest - a "virtual pinboard" social networking site.
