Raizlabs worked with the leader in swapping, Swap.com to design, develop and launch the Swap iPhone app. The app released last week has been a hit with users giving the application an initial 5 star average rating in its first week.

Swap.com has over a million members who will now be able to scan, books, CD's, games & DVD's. Users of Swap trade items they want for items they have.

Founder of Raizlabs, Gregory Raiz, said: "We wanted to take a great service and make it even better. Mobile is the perfect tool to scan bar-codes and initiate trades. I couldn't be happier with the initial launch. This is going to really revolutionize the Swap.com business."

The application is available for free for on the Apple iTunes Store.
