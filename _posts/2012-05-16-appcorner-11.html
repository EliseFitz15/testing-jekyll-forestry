In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk about the speculation over the next MacBook Pro update, discuss Apple's upcoming Worldwide Developer's Conference in June, new iCloud features coming soon, Yahoo's CEO ousting debacle and as usual, our app recommendations of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Andrew Tremblay (@drewlooksround), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • Pxl - allows you to create abstract compositions out of your own photos.
  • Stereo - turns any two iPhone, iPad or iPod touch devices into a TRUE stereo sound system.
  • 123D Catch - automatically turns your photos into amazing 3D models.
  • Chess With Friends - play chess with your friends and family anytime, anywhere.
  • Osmosis - a puzzle game for iPhone and iPad.
