In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk about the pros and cons of Instagram (now on iPhone and Android platforms), Microsoft's Kinect technology, Google and Apple failures of the past, and our app recommendations of the week - with bonus DJing!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jason Petralia (@jasonpetralia), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog), Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle) and Craig Spitzkoff (@oofoot).

AppCorner: Episode #5

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • Cinemagr.am - animate a small portion of a photo to create awesome .gif images. See an example.
  • djay for iPad - DJ app for iPad.
  • Paper - drawing app for iPad.
  • Zombie Gunship - zombie shooter game for iPhone and iPad.
  • Comcast Xfinity for Android - Comcast mobile app that connects to your TV, voicemail and e-mail.
