In this week's episode of AppCorner we talk about the IKEA Uppleva, Path's latest $30 million round of funding, and of course, our recommendations for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Rich Stern (@richstern), Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

AppCorner: Episode #7

Apps discussed in this episode include:

  • IMDB - the internet movie database.
  • Figure - a music making app powered by Reason.
  • MoMA - the official iPhone app for the Museum of Modern Art.
  • Soulcaliber - the SEGA Dreamcast game comes to iOS.
  • Robot Unicorn Attack - an endless runner game made by Adult Swim.
