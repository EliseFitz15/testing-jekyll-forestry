In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about Apple's latest patent lawsuit, The Guardian's article We've passed peak Apple: it's all downhill from here and our first impressions after getting our hands on the new iPad Mini and Microsoft's Surface tablet. Of course, we also recommend our picks for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's regular podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jonathan Green (@gildedsplinter) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Just Landed - Just Landed™ intelligently updates itself using your current location and the latest flight and traffic information. Just go about your day and the app will tell you when it's time to head for the airport. Never be late to pick someone up again!
  • Star Wars Arcade: Falcon Gunner - Inspired by the classic 1984 Star Wars arcade title, this first-person action game throws you into the Millennium Falcon's gunnery seat, letting you experience more than 20 levels of arcade action spanning the legendary smuggling vessel's most exciting moments -- including the Death Star escape, asteroid field pursuit across Hoth and infamous Kessel Run.
  • Bit Pilot - Bit Pilot uses an intuitive and unique control scheme unlike anything you've tried before. Each thumb swipe on the screen adds the force of that swipe to your ship. Control with one thumb for small motions, and use two at once in the same direction for quick movements, or two in different directions for precision. Its difficult to explain, but extremely intuitive and precise once you try it. Check out the videos on bitpilotgame.com.
  • Super Hexagon -  Players contros a small triangle near the eye of a spiraling maze, a whirling, colorful, psychedelic bathtub drain of barriers and pathways that swiftly converge toward a central point. The player must safely glide the triangle through the ever-tightening maze. Should the triangle bump into any wall, game over.
  • Bastion - Bastion for iPad features all the highly acclaimed artwork, music, and narration that Bastion is known for, along with intuitive touch controls. Explore more than 40 lush hand-painted environments as you discover the secrets of the Calamity, a surreal catastrophe that shattered the world to pieces. Wield a huge arsenal of upgradeable weapons and battle savage beasts adapted to their new habitat. Finish the main story to unlock New Game Plus mode and continue your journey!
