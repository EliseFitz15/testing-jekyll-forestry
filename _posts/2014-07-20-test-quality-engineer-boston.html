Raizlabs is looking for a talented Software Test / Quality Engineer to drive the quality of our products across iOS, Android and Web. We strive for excellence in every pixel and line of code, and you will hold us to that standard. We expect you to help shape product design and direction with your testing. The ideal candidate has significant functional software testing knowledge and chops, preferably with experience in mobile. Automation and development experience are pluses. Sound like you?

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Identify and document desired application behavior in collaboration with representatives from product management, software engineering, and design.
  • Design and author automated unit and UI tests.
  • Perform routine regression and performance audits on active projects.
  • Administer and extend our continuous integration system.
  • Assist engineering with application debugging and performance profiling as necessary.
  • Explore opportunities to improve company efficiency and product quality through technology and innovation.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree preferred
  • Superb written communication skills
  • Development experience in iOS, Android, and/or Web a strong plus
  • Experience with quality process and methodologies another huge plus
  • Passion for creating awesome applications

Location: Boston (no offsite work or contractors). Principals only. Recruiters, please don’t bother contacting us and do not re-post. Qualified applicants should submit a resume and cover letter.
