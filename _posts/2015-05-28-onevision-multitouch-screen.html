Innovating New Digital Frontiers

OneVision, a managed services provider, came to Raizlabs with a client's dream of converting their analog, paper maps to a digital, interactive interface - Minority Report-style. Together we conceptualized a high-definition digital interface that was simple enough to be used with one hand but comprehensive enough to include everything their paper maps delivered and more.

As an early partner, Raizlabs played a key role in identifying the technology used in the project - from the latest Mac Pro to the latest 4K display technology, we performed thorough feasibility tests to ensure that the end result we all wanted was possible.
