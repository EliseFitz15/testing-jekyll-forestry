A Sky-high Challenge

Virgin Pulse, part of Richard Branson's Virgin Group, supplies employers with software that support employee fitness. The company approached Raizlabs to be a development partner for its GoZone 3 BTLE device, a pedometer with wireless synching capability slated for an initial run of over 100,000 devices.

Getting Results

Raizlabs worked to design a wireless, (BTLE) Bluetooth low energy solution to sync the GoZone 3 device with the iPhone, successfully building a device as proof of concept. As a result, Virgin Pulse was able to begin full-scale production on the device.

Raizlabs worked with Virgin to take the BTLE solution produced for the iPhone and extend the solution to work on Android as well.
