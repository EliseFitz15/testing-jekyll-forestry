Rue La La has been named the "Mobile Merchandiser of the Year" by Mobile Commerce Daily. The company's mobile application, designed and developed by Raizlabs, allows users to shop the exclusive flash sale website with ease while providing an exceptional boutique shopping experience. Rue La La's app was previously named the "2011 Best Mobile Shopping App" by Mobile Marketing Association.

"This year, Rue La La has done an impeccable job ensuring its mobile shoppers have an enjoyable experience when searching for products," commented Mobile Commerce Daily. "Rue La La's mobile merchandising techniques help drive the company's sales." Rue currently reports that more than 40 percent of their sales come from mobile devices. Rue La La are the 3rd fastest-growing and 19th largest app in Mobile Commerce Platform by Internet Retailer on the 2013 Mobile 400 ranking.
