For the second year in a row, Raizlabs has been named on the Inc. 5000 list as one of the fastest-growing private companies in the nation.

The Inc. 5000 is ranked in accordance with percentage revenue growth over a three-year period.  This year, Inc. ranked Raizlabs at #1976 with our growth checking in at 200 percent. “We are super excited to be recognized by Inc. for the second consecutive year,” said Greg Raiz, Owner and CEO. “We are proud to be growing as fast as we have been while still producing great software and maintaining our amazing culture and team.”

Raizlabs is a national leader in mobile applications and mobile design. The company has helped numerous startups and Fortune 100 companies with their mobile strategy and execution.

The 2015 Inc. 5000, unveiled online at Inc.com and with the top 500 companies featured in the September issue of Inc. is the most competitive crop in the list’s history. The average company on the list achieved a three-year growth of 490%. The Inc. 5000’s aggregate revenue is $205 billion, generating 647,000 jobs over the past three years.
