In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat all about music apps, inspired by the launch of the "new" Myspace which is heavily focused around music now. As always, we also pick our favorite apps and recommend them to listeners.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's regular podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jonathan Green (@gildedsplinter), Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle) and Nick Donaldson.

[display_podcast]

Apps discussed this week included:

  • doubleTwist for Android - Download doubleTwist Player and get the best all-in-one music, podcast, radio, video & sync app. Sync music (including iTunes playlists) to your phone from your Mac/PC over USB or AirSync over WiFi. Download legal, high-quality album artwork for your music. Subscribe to podcasts without needing a Mac/PC and get new episodes downloaded automatically over 3G or WiFi. Stream music, videos and photos to the Xbox 360, PS3, Sonos, and Apple TV (black model; AirPort Express not yet supported).
  • Thicket - Thicket is an audiovisual playground that allows anyone to create beautiful sounds and pictures from simple finger touches. For users of all ages, this generative art and sound app immerses you in song-like audiovisual pieces called modes.
  • bandcamper - Introducing bandcamper. A fast, free Bandcamp music streamer and artist browser for iPhone and iPod Touch. Features include: music streaming, store your favorite albums or singles, search by artist name, share albums with friends via Twitter or Facebook, view track/album info and more.
  • SoundCloud - SoundCloud is the world’s leading social sound platform, with over ten hours of music & audio posted every minute by the world’s largest community of artists, bands, podcasters and other creators. With the SoundCloud app for iPhone and iPad, you can hear anything, wherever you are. Featuring an enhanced UI, new search capabilities and a Repost feature that helps you share your favourite music and audio, the updated app lets you stream music at home or on the go over 3G or WiFi.
  • Relax - The Deep Relaxation App is the perfect way to unwind and de-stress, and it also has had great success in therapeutic settings. The App teaches relaxation techniques, which have helped with ME, pain control, insomnia, post traumatic stress disorder and many other stress symptoms.
  • Downcast - Download and listen to your favorite podcasts directly from your iOS device without the need to sync with iTunes.
