In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about the world of e-commerce and mobile shopping. Apple's iPad led the way on Black Friday this year, taking 10% of all online shopping - more than any other tablet or smartphone.  Mobile shopping in general rose 20.7% on Black Friday from 2011. We also discuss General Motor's announcement they will be bringing Siri integration to their Chevy Sonic and Spark cars next year. Of course, we also recommend our picks (or deletions) for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's regular podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Dave Counts (@davecounts), Jonathan Green (@gildedsplinter) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Fix-It Felix Jr. - Disney and Wreck-It Ralph present Fix-It Felix Jr! If there's something to break, there's something to fix and this game's got a lot of both! Journey back to Wreck-it Ralph’s 8-bit origins in a brand new game with classic arcade looks and fun. Play as Fix-it Felix Jr. and use your magical hammer to fix whatever Ralph destroys! After all, it’s what you’ve been programmed to do.
  • Scratch Hard - Want to save time and money when you're out shopping? Tired of using your phone’s browser to search for products and stores? You need Scratch Hard – a mobile shopping assistant and location-based search engine which helps you find products, brands, and stores when and where you’re looking for them. Plan your shopping trip ahead or uncover cool stuff and great savings on the go.
  • Amazon Mobile - Browse, search, get product details, read reviews and purchase millions of products available from Amazon.com and other merchants. Sign in with your existing Amazon account to access your cart, payment and shipping options. No need to create a new account to manage your 1-Click settings and wish lists, track your orders and use your Prime membership benefits. Shop just as you do on the web.
  • TED - ED's official app presents talks from some of the world's most fascinating people: education radicals, tech geniuses, medical mavericks, business gurus and music legends. Find more than 1,100 TEDTalk videos (with more added each week) on the official TED app -- now for both iPad and iPhone.
