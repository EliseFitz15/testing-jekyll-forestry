Raizlabs has just released a Facebook plug-in for the recently introduced PicMe Photo Sharing platform. The new plug-in allows users to invite, share and manage their photo content from their desktop. The PicMe application, available from http://picme.raizlabs.com was recently introduced and has been gaining popularity as a desktop based photo sharing tool.

The application now supports Facebook, Flickr and additional plug-ins are in the works. The PicMe software allows users to share hundreds of photos directly from their desktop. Basic and premium services are also available. The software is available as a free download from: http://picme.raizlabs.com
