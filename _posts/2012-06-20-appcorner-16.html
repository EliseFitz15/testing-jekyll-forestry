In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about Linus Torvald's (creator of Linux) message to Nvidia, the un-fixable, unhackable nature of the new next-gen Macbook Pro's with retina display, the Microsoft Surface and whether this will make any dent in the tablet market, and John Carmack's prototype 3D head mounted display. As usual we close out our podcast with app recommendations of the week.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Craig Spitzkoff (@oofoot), Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle) and Bruno Germansderfer (@brunog).

Apps discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Just Landed - intelligently updates itself based on your current location and the latest flight and traffic information. Just go about your day and the app will let you know when it's time to head for the airport.
  • Figure Runner - a new sport based on GPS technology. Get creative and discover new places while running and getting fit. This app enables you to make your own GPS art. It includes features like coloured pencils, an eraser, adjustable drawing precision and easy sharing. The aim is not to run faster or longer, but to run with more fun.
  • Quizboard - new head-to-head social trivia game. Challenge your friends to a battle of wits and show off your knowledge of everything from Pop Culture to Popcorn. Master the strategies of 10 different boards where every move you make can take your opponent in a different direction
  • Level Up - the pay phone app. Pay with your phone. Register for LevelUp to get your unique LevelUp Code and link it to your debit/credit card. Simply scan your code to pay at your favorite merchants. You'll get an instant digital receipt. Users unlock rewards whenever you go back to merchants.
