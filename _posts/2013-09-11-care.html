How it Happened

Raizlabs identified the existing app's mix of mobile web and native iOS code as a potential problem, so we started over from scratch and built an entirely native iPhone application, making sure to carry forward the existing features with which current users were familiar.

Making Meaningful Connections

By focusing on the specific mobile experience of a family seeking a caregiver, Raizlabs was able to find new ways to make the app easy to use and continually engage users over time. As a result, more families and caregivers connect on Care.com and remain there rather than dropping off the platform.
