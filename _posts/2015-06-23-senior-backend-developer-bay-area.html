BACKEND ENGINEERS THAT MAKE MOBILE APPS POSSIBLE.

Do more than code. Raizlabs is seeking an experienced Senior Backend Developer to engineer backend services and APIs that will make it possible for our mobile teams to build beautiful mobile experiences.

You will have influence over product direction for startups and big brands like Bloomingdale’s, Localytics, RunKeeper, Rue La La, Care.com, and HubSpot.

You’ll be working with enthusiastic and supportive peers in a trust-based work environment. In fact, you’ll be helping to keep it that way. As an early employee of our Backend team, you will strongly influence our culture, priorities, and future hiring.

Like what you’ve read so far?


Here’s more on what you’d be doing and some things that would make you successful at Raizlabs:

THINGS OUR BACKEND DEVELOPERS DO:

  • Work as part of a Scrum/Agile based team environment to achieve sprint deliverables and acceptance criteria.
  • Communicate with clients about scope and needs
  • Learn new technologies and teach teammates.
  • Document & consult client API requirements.

ABILITIES AND TRAITS OF OUR TEAM MEMBERS:

  • Ability to guide clients to create thoughtful, scalable, and sustainable feature sets.
  • Ability to work with Designers, Product Managers, and QA Engineers to quickly turn around requirements.
  • Ability to lead projects from start to ship.
  • Analytical decision making skills

YOU HAVE EXPERIENCE WITH:

  • Ruby & Go (or proven ability in at least two modern languages).
  • Implementing amazing interfaces on mobile and desktop.
  • Setting up and maintaining development and production environments.
  • Deep understanding of architecting new, existing, and scalable API’s.

WHAT OUR WORK ENVIRONMENT OFFERS IN RETURN:

  • Full coverage of health and dental insurance premiums for full time employees.
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Trust-based vacation policy (take what you need).
  • Conferences and Events policy to support continued professional growth
  • Frequent tech talks, team lunches, hack days for open exploration, weekly coffee and beer tastings, and our mobile meet-up, “Drinks on Tap.”

Location: Oakland, CA (no offsite work or contractors). Principals only. Recruiters, please don’t bother contacting us and do not re-post. Qualified applicants submit a resume and cover letter.
