In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about the hackers who broke into an F.B.I. agent's computer and extracted over a million Apple UDID's and chat about Apple's official iPhone 5 event announcement, set for September 12th. Of course, we also recommend our picks (or deletions) for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Jon Green (@gildedsplinter), Dave Counts (@davecounts) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).
