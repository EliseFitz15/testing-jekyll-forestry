In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about NBC's Olympic's media fail at streaming and providing live coverage, iPhone 5 release date and rumors, and the appointment of Marissa Mayer as Yahoo's new CEO. Mayer previous served as a long-time executive and key spokesperson for Google.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Craig Spitzkoff (@oofoot), Alex Rouse (@arrouse88), Michelle Chen (@myichen) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • ArtSpotter - Your free guide that lets you discover and interact with art - wherever you are. Find nearby galleries and exhibitions, add your photos and new events to the map, and share the art exhibitions you love with your friends on Twitter and Facebook.
  • Word Lens - See the world in your language: instantly translate printed words using your built-in video camera, in real time! Word Lens gives you translation on the go.
  • Nike+ FuelBand - Nike+ FuelBand measures your everyday activity and turns it into NikeFuel. It also tracks each step and calorie burned. The App talks to your Nike+ FuelBand, allowing you to see your progress on your mobile device and get the motivation you need to get moving.
  • London 2012 Olympics Official Results App - Provides all the latest news, schedules and results, allowing users to keep up-to-date with the latest action LIVE across all Olympic sports (25 July to 12 August 2012). An up-to-date list of all athletes participating in the Olympic Games, including individual biographies, related news stories and their personal performance at London 2012. You can search for athletes by sport and by country, and select individuals as favorites in the My Games section of the app. Key features include results, live updates, calendar schedule, details of sports, medal tables and athlete profiles. Users can also follow specific countries, and receive official news and updates tailored to them all in one app.
  • Parkbud - The most comprehensive, beautifully designed parking & navigation app on the market. Find nearby parking garages, pay for parking anywhere in the world where mobile payments are accepted, keep a library of parking payment data so you can pay/renew anywhere, find nearby & favorite points of interest (Gas, Food, Repair), track meter time & car location, get reminders when parking time is expired, get up to the minute directions back to your car and more!
