In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about GitHub's $100 million series A round of funding they just received, the new Dropbox pricing plans and pros/cons of storage and cloud services, and as usual our app recommendations of the week.

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Ben Johnson (@scoober), Dave Counts (@davecounts) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

[display_podcast]

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Music for Programming podcast - A series of mixes intended for listening while programming to aid concentration and increase productivity.
  • Hooked on Words - Form words in any direction to clear that word from the board and then watch as new letters fall in their place. Create bigger words to earn bonus tiles that can help extend your game and achieve bigger scores. With its 4 game modes there is a little something for everyone whether you like to take your time and strategize or prefer fast and furious action.
  • OpenTable - Free, Instant, Confirmed. With OpenTable for iPhone you can make free restaurant reservations at more than 20,000 OpenTable-enabled restaurants in the United States and Canada.
  • Dark Sky - Using GPS, Dark Sky is able to create forecasts for your precise location. And because Dark Sky focuses on the next hour, the app is able to give you minute-to-minute predictions. You can also explore the most beautiful weather animation you’ve ever seen, both forward in time or back.
  • White Noise Box - Features all of kinds of quality noise samples, helping you sleep, reduce stress, increase privacy, mask your tinitus, increase your concentration or simply block distractions. This application features high fidelity white, red (brown), violet, pink and blue noise loops, as well as various nature sounds.
  • card.io - The card.io SDK is a drop-in library for iOS or Android. The SDK provides a fast, simple, delightful user experience of paying with a credit card and easy deployment of credit card scanning in mobile and web apps.
