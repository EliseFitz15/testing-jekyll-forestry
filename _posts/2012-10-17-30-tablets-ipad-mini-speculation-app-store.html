In this week's episode of AppCorner we chat about the new Apple event announced for next week, speculate about the iPad Mini pricing + possible other hardware updates and voice our frustrations over the new App Store in iOS6 and why it's such a problem. Of course, we also recommend our picks (or deletions) for app of the week!

AppCorner is hosted by the team at Raizlabs. This week's podcast crew included Greg Raiz (@graiz), Jon Green (@gildedsplinter), Dave Counts (@davecounts) and Michelle Buchman (@tres_michelle).

Apps and other cool things discussed in this weeks podcast include:

  • Downcast - Download and listen to your favorite podcasts directly from your iOS device without the need to sync with iTunes. Subscription features include: search, subscribe to and download audio/video podcasts of any size, manually subscribe via URL, automatic download, auto-update feeds, create and edit "smart" playlists, support for password-protected feeds and more. Playback features include: video and audio AirPlay support, chapters support for enhanced podcasts, stream episodes without downloading, control audio with Bluetooth or other headphone remote controls, gesture support, sleep timer and more.
  • Weather 2x - Designed to look stunning on the retina display on both the iPad and iPhone, Weather 2x is an entirely new and innovative way to view weather conditions for the locations that matter to you most. Features include: stunning weather visuals featuring subtle animations, 5 day forecast, current weather conditions, track, manager and maintain unlimited weather locations around the globe, wind speed and direction details, extended information including precipitation, visibility, humidity and pressure level details, "local weather" feature automatically updates based on current location, hourly forecast detail and more!
  • Fab - Fab is the #1 rated shopping app for design. More than 7 million people turn to Fab for everyday design across all categories, at all price points. The Fab app for your iPhone or iPad gives you access 24/7 anywhere you are.
  • App.net - App.net is a real-time social feed without the ads. It’s your real-time feed, a home for meaningful conversation, where you control your data. Find the apps you love and make App.net your own. Connect, play, and discover.
  • NetBot - Netbot is a full-featured iPhone (and iPod touch) App.net client with a lot of personality. Whether it’s the meticulously-crafted interface, sounds & animation, or features like smart gestures, there’s a lot to love about Netbot.
