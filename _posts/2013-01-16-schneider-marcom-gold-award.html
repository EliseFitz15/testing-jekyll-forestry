The Schneider Electric White Paper mobile application has won a 2012 MARCOM Gold award in the Website Element/Mobile App category. The MarCom Awards are a creative competition for any individual or company involved in the concept, writing and design of print, visual, audio and web materials and programs. The competition is well regarded in the marketing and communications industry with entrants including national well-known organizations and businesses.

Raizlabs developed the PDF viewer mobile app with Schneider Electric to bring users access to more than $90 million worth of free research articles in the companies extensive white paper library. Schneider Electric are a Fortune Global 500 electric engineering company specializing in intelligent energy management.

Download the Schneider Electric White Paper app in the App Store.
